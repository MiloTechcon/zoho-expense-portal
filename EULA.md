- **"Customer"** means the legal entity identified in the signature block.

- **"Licenced Software"** means the Image, the Licence Key, and any updates or new versions of the Image that Techcon publishes during the Term.
- **"Customer Data"** means all data uploaded to or generated by the Licenced Software while it is operated by the Customer, including receipt images, expense records, user accounts, and configuration values.

## 5. Term and termination

5.1 **Initial Term.** This Agreement commences on the date of the Customer's signature below and continues for an initial term of **one (1) year** (the **"Initial Term"**).

5.2 **Renewal.** This Agreement automatically renews for successive one-year terms (each a **"Renewal Term"**, and together with the Initial Term, the **"Term"**) upon payment of the applicable renewal fee, unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least **thirty (30) days** before the end of the then-current term.

5.3 **Termination for convenience.** Either party may terminate this Agreement with **thirty (30) days'** written notice.

5.4 **Termination for cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately on written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and does not cure the breach within fifteen (15) days of being notified of it.

5.5 **Effect of Licence Key expiry.** The Licence Key contains an embedded expiry date (`exp` claim). When that date passes, the Licenced Software ceases to start, **regardless of the contractual status of this Agreement**. The Customer acknowledges that the Licence Key's expiry, and not the Term, is the technical control that limits operation of the Licenced Software.

5.6 **Effect of termination.** On termination, the Customer's right to use the Licenced Software ceases. The Customer may continue to access Customer Data via its own database backups (which it owns under Section 4) but shall stop running the Image after the Licence Key expires.
